SeqA / RIDA

SeqA sequestration window: 10 min (~0.33 of doubling time at rapid growth). SeqA binds hemimethylated GATC sites (>10 per origin).

RIDA: DnaN (β-clamp) + ADP-Hda (N-terminal clamp-binding motif) catalyzes DnaA-ATP -> DnaA-ADP.

Consensus motifs

Consensus: TTWTNCACA (W = A|T, N = any). Highest-affinity 9-mer: TTATCCACA (Kd ~1 nM).

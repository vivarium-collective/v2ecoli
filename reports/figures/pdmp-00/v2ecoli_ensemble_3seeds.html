v2ecoli baseline — 3-seed ensemble (Phase 0 ensemble prototype)

First inter-replicate spread experiment for v2ecoli's WCM. Three independent stochastic runs on M9-glucose with seeds 0/1/2, 30 steps each (~3 min wall clock per seed, ~9 min total). Same 4 observables as the 100-step single run, now with per-seed lines + the N=3 mean +/- standard- deviation envelope.

The CV (coefficient of variation, std/|mean|) at the final step quantifies stochastic spread. At N=3 these CV numbers are noisy estimates; the Phase 0 reference ensemble at N>=64 will sharpen them by sqrt(64/3) ~ 4.6x. The shape of the variance envelope here is what later phases will compare against via W2 distance per observable.

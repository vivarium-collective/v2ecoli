Each violin shows the distribution of steady-state growth rate µ across N=64 independent stochastic replicates of v2ecoli's WCM, in one of three media conditions. The width of each violin is the inter-replicate stochasticity; MCSE (Monte Carlo standard error on the ensemble mean) scales as σ/√N — for N=64 and σ ≈ 0.06 1/h, MCSE ≈ 0.008 1/h, below the cross-condition effect size (0.36 → 1.12, ≈ 3× difference). This is the gating criterion for Phase 0: the reference ensemble must reach MCSE < 10% of inter-condition effect size on every observable the Phase 1+ studies will validate against.

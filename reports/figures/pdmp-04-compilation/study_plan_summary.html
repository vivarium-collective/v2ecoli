Catalyst.jl + column-centric runtime achieves ≥10³ parallel single-cell trajectories with W₂(p_compiled, p_phase2) ≤ 5·σ_MC. Marshalling overhead < 10% of per-step compute. 1/√N posterior variance scaling holds on ABC test posteriors. FP summation order deterministic (pairwise/Kahan). Per-step compute orders-of-magnitude above v2ecoli baseline.

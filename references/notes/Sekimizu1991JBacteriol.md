# Sekimizu1991JBacteriol — Original DnaA protein abundance measurement

**Sekimizu, Yung, Kornberg, 1991, J Bacteriol 173(16):5194-5199.**
[https://journals.asm.org/doi/abs/10.1128/jb.173.16.5194-5199.1991](https://journals.asm.org/doi/abs/10.1128/jb.173.16.5194-5199.1991)

## Why it matters here

Earliest quantitative western-blot measurement of DnaA monomer counts per
cell. The plan cites this as "the original DnaA protein abundance
measurement"; modern mass-spec values should agree within an order of
magnitude.

## Key numbers (to extract on read)

- DnaA monomer count per cell at slow / fast growth
- Membrane-bound vs cytoplasmic split

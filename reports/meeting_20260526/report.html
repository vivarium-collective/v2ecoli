Mechanism summary

FBA over-supplies AAs early gen ↓ tRNA stays 97-98% charged ↓ SpoT degrades ppGpp freely → ppGpp settles at ~23 µM ↓ ribosomes elongate at ~21 aa/s ↓ mass grows ~3× in 80 min → D-period trigger → division at 81 min | | (proteome drifts slowly through the cycle) ↓ ppGpp climbs 23 → 67 µM in late gen — partial recovery
Same crash-then-recover pattern in both architectures; the gap between observed τ (~80 min) and declared τ (136 min) is upstream — wcEcoli-inherited, not introduced by the v2ecoli port.

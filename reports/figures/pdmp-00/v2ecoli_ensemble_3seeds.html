v2ecoli baseline 3-seed ensemble — DEGENERATE (open issue)

⚠ BUG SURFACED: 3 seeds produced bit-identical trajectories. The dashboard's run-runner correctly propagates overrides.seed = 0/1/2 into the composite generator (verified in request.json), and v2ecoli/composites/baseline.py:408 DOES seed the allocator_rng with that seed. But every other stochastic process in the 55-process WCM has its own RNG (transcription, translation, division timing, protein degradation, equilibrium, two-component-system, ...) — those RNGs are seeded from the ParCa cache, which is shared across all three runs. Net result: zero variance across the ensemble.

The 4 panels show all 3 seed trajectories overlapped (markers + lines) — they're literally on top of each other, hence the ΔCV = 0.000% annotation. This is what's happening, honestly labelled. Before Phase 0's reference ensemble (3 conditions × N≥64 replicates) is meaningful, the per-process RNG seeding needs to be fixed: each of the stochastic processes (allocator, transcript-elongation, polypeptide-elongation, division-threshold, equilibrium, ...) needs to seed its RNG from a hash of (master_seed, process_name) so that distinct master seeds yield truly distinct trajectories.

Notes

The discrete (legacy) mode samples per-promoter event counts with one multinomial(n_target, p) draw — exact sum constraint Σ Ni = n_target, promoters coupled through that constraint. The Poisson mode treats each promoter as an independent jump process at rate λi = n_target · pi; this is the per-promoter marginal a continuous-time PDMP integrates against (the Phase-3 likelihood target). Per-tick totals fluctuate by O(√n_target), the per-TU rates converge to the same expectation, and the cell_mass evolution stays within stochastic noise of the discrete baseline.

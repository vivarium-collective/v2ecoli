PLANNED: pdmp-02 stochastic gene-expression trajectories

⚠ PLANNED COMPARISON - synthetic data, layout & thresholds only
Single-cell stochastic trajectories of mRNA + protein counts for 5 representative genes, 4 replicates each. The replicate spread per gene is the inter-cell variability the Phase 2 PDMP should reproduce. The current v2ecoli scaled-Poisson approach produces wider spread than the literature warrants (gene-expression noise too high); the continuous-time jump-process replacement should bring the CV back into the published 0.2-0.4 range per gene.

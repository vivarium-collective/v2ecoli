Where Phase 3 stands

Phase 3's stated scope is "observation likelihoods + likelihood accumulator + Vivarium observe/intervene effects; ABC-SMC baseline". Sprints 1–9 deliver the full mechanical loop and validate it on synthetic data:

  • Likelihoods per Phase-2 jump process (sprints 1, 2).
  • Aggregate collector step writing listeners.likelihood.total (sprint 2).
  • Persistence via XArrayEmitter to per-replicate zarr stores (sprint 4, after sprint 3's SQLite misstep).
  • Ensemble-shaped readback as xarray.Dataset(replicate × time × observable) — the canonical inference data shape (sprint 4).
  • Reviewable figure with provenance banner (sprint 5).
  • Calibrated noise floor — pairwise (sprint 6) and mean-to-mean (sprint 8) — setting the ABC acceptance ε empirically.
  • One-parameter forward-model ABC stub on transcript_init_prob_scale, distinguishing truth from perturbations (sprint 7).
  • SMC sequential refinement showing posterior concentration around the truth as ε tightens (sprint 9).

Closeout — SMC posterior collapses to the truth

At p25 and p05 the posterior contains only scale = 1.0, the synthetic truth. Each ε step monotonically eliminates the most extreme remaining proposal — classic SMC refinement. The Phase-2 closeout finding (cell_mass is the wrong observable; count- level listeners carry the jump-process signal) is now operationally actionable: a real ABC-SMC driver can read the persisted per-tick likelihood.total directly from disk and invert transcript-side parameters with no further infrastructure changes.

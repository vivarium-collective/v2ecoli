Simulation-Based Calibration (Talts et al. 2018): for K=1000 synthetic (θ*, y*) draws from the prior + forward PDMP, run ABC-SMC to obtain a posterior sample, record the rank of the true θ* in the posterior sample. Under correct calibration the rank histogram is uniform — Poisson 95% band shown grey. A non-uniform histogram signals miscalibration of the ABC-SMC posterior (likely sources: insufficient particles, ε-schedule too aggressive, summary-statistic covariance ill-conditioned).
